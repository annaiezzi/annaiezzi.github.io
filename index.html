Hey there! Welcome to my personal homepage!

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the Department of Mathematics & Statistics of the University of South Florida, in Tampa.
In my research I am interested in algebraic geometry over finite fields (algebraic curves, number of rational points, zeta functions, function fields, etc.) and its application to cryptography and coding theory.

I did my Ph.D thesis at the Institut de Mathématiques de Marseille of Aix-Marseille Université under the supervision of Yves Aubry.

Am I obsessed by $\pi$? Maybe... or maybe this is just because I also am a founder member and secretary of the Pi day association. The Pi day association, which wants to promote and communicate Mathematics to the general public, has been awarded the d'Alembert price in 2016 by the Société Mathématique de France. Within this context, I coordinated the organisation of several important events in 2015, 2016 and 2017.

Enjoy the surf experience through my website, where you can find more information about my research, my teaching and my activities of scientific popularization!
